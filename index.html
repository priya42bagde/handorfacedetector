Hand or Face Detector

Description :

Hand Detector is useful to detects the moving of hands nearby face which is higly not recommended in this era of COVID. The intention is to illustrate a (very) simple way to utilize AI and ML to detect hands and face. The ability to perceive the shape and motion of hands can be a vital component in improving the user experience across a variety of technological domains and platforms. For example, it can form the basis for sign language understanding and hand gesture control.
